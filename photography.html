Photography

In my freetime i really enjoy taking pictures of pretty much everything. It ranges from nature over people to architecture.

This project is more of a personal project, than a uni project. I started taking a few pictures in Australia with someone elses camera and then bought one for myself in summer 2019. Since then i go out into the city sometimes and take pictures of lots of different things. After that i go home and edit the best ones in Photoshop or Lightroom. Once i am happy with thea outcome i upload them to my instagram account, which you find in the contact section or the footer of my website.
