User Trialing

This project was for my course "Research for Design", where I had to do user testings with people on the website of the Rijksmueum in Amsterdam.

For the project me and my group had to create a plan for how we are going to conduct the user testings. Once that was done, we did a pilot testing on ourselves, to figure out if any questions or tasks for the people could be improved. After that we invited one person each from different age groups for the user testing. We also checked, that we have users, who are interested in art and some that aren't. When they came along we did the testing with every single one of them, as well as an interview afterwards. At the end we gathered our notes, transcripts and observations and created different charts, to see, how well different users did on the tasks.
